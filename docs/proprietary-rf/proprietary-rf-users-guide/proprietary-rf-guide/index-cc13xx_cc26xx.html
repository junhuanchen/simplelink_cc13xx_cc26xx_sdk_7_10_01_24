Proprietary RF User’s Guide¶

This document describes how to use the SimpleLink Wireless MCU family CC13xx and CC26xx from a software developer’s point of view. The focus is on proprietary communication on the RF core, but some general topics about TI-RTOS7 the high-level drivers are also covered.
