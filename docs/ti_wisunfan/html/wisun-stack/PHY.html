Data Rate and PHY¶

PHYs (also known as Modes) are defined by Wi-SUN Alliance. The following modes are supported by TI Wi-SUN FAN Stack. (All modes use 2-FSK.)

Wi-SUN frequency bands:

  • EU: Europe

  • NA: North America

  • JP: Japan

  • BZ: Brazil
