#define ATTRID_POWER_CONFIGURATION_MAINS_VOLTAGE   0x0000

The MainsVoltage attribute is 16 bits in length and specifies the actual (measured) RMS voltage (or DC voltage in the 3501 case of a DC supply) currently applied to the device, measured in units of 100mV.

§ ATTRID_POWER_CONFIGURATION_MAINS_VOLTAGE_DWELL_TRIP_POINT

#define ATTRID_POWER_CONFIGURATION_MAINS_VOLTAGE_DWELL_TRIP_POINT   0x0013

The MainsVoltageDwellTripPoint attribute is 16 bits in length and specifies the length of time, in seconds that the 3545 value of MainsVoltage MAY exist beyond either of its thresholds before an alarm is generated.

§ ATTRID_POWER_CONFIGURATION_MAINS_VOLTAGE_MAX_THRESHOLD

#define ATTRID_POWER_CONFIGURATION_MAINS_VOLTAGE_MAX_THRESHOLD   0x0012

The MainsVoltageMaxThreshold attribute is 16 bits in length and specifies the upper alarm threshold, measured in units of 100mV, for the MainsVoltage attribute.

§ ATTRID_POWER_CONFIGURATION_MAINS_VOLTAGE_MIN_THRESHOLD

#define ATTRID_POWER_CONFIGURATION_MAINS_VOLTAGE_MIN_THRESHOLD   0x0011

The MainsVoltageMinThreshold attribute is 16 bits in length and specifies the lower alarm threshold, measured in units of 100mV, for the MainsVoltage attribute.
