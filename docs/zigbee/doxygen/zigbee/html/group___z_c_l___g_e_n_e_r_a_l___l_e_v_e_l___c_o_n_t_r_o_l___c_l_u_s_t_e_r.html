Macro Definition Documentation

§ ATTRID_LEVEL_CURRENT_FREQUENCY

#define ATTRID_LEVEL_CURRENT_FREQUENCY   0x0004

The CurrentFrequency attribute represents the frequency that the devices is at CurrentLevel. A CurrentFrequency of 0 is unknown.

§ ATTRID_LEVEL_CURRENT_LEVEL

#define ATTRID_LEVEL_CURRENT_LEVEL   0x0000

The CurrentLevel attribute represents the current level of this device. The meaning of 'level' is device dependent.

§ ATTRID_LEVEL_DEFAULT_MOVE_RATE

#define ATTRID_LEVEL_DEFAULT_MOVE_RATE   0x0014

The DefaultMoveRate attribute determines the movement rate, in units per second, when a Move command is received with a Rate parameter of 0xFF.

§ ATTRID_LEVEL_MAX_FREQUENCY

#define ATTRID_LEVEL_MAX_FREQUENCY   0x0006

The MaxFrequency attribute indicates the maximum value of CurrentFrequency that is capable of being assigned. MaxFrequency shall be greater than or equal to MinFrequency. A value of 0 indicates undefined.

§ ATTRID_LEVEL_MAX_LEVEL

#define ATTRID_LEVEL_MAX_LEVEL   0x0003

The MaxLevel attribute indicates the maximum value of CurrentLevel that is capable of being assigned.

§ ATTRID_LEVEL_MIN_FREQUENCY

#define ATTRID_LEVEL_MIN_FREQUENCY   0x0005

The MinFrequency attribute indicates the minimum value of CurrentFrequency that is capable of being assigned. MinFrequency shall be less than or equal to MaxFrequency. A value of 0 indicates undefined.

§ ATTRID_LEVEL_MIN_LEVEL

#define ATTRID_LEVEL_MIN_LEVEL   0x0002

The MinLevel attribute indicates the minimum value of CurrentLevel that is capable of being assigned.

§ ATTRID_LEVEL_OFF_TRANSITION_TIME

#define ATTRID_LEVEL_OFF_TRANSITION_TIME   0x0013

The OffTransitionTime attribute represents the time taken to move the current level from the maximum level to the minimum level when an Off command is received by an On/Off cluster on the same endpoint.

§ ATTRID_LEVEL_ON_LEVEL

#define ATTRID_LEVEL_ON_LEVEL   0x0011

The OnLevel attribute determines the value that the CurrentLevel attribute is set to when the OnOff attribute of an On/Off cluster on the same endpoint is set to On, as a result of processing an On/Off cluster command.

§ ATTRID_LEVEL_ON_OFF_TRANSITION_TIME

#define ATTRID_LEVEL_ON_OFF_TRANSITION_TIME   0x0010

The OnOffTransitionTime attribute represents the time taken to move to or from the target level when On of Off commands are received by an On/Off cluster on the same endpoint. It is specified in 1/10ths of a second.

§ ATTRID_LEVEL_ON_TRANSITION_TIME

#define ATTRID_LEVEL_ON_TRANSITION_TIME   0x0012

The OnTransitionTime attribute represents the time taken to move the current level from the minimum level to the maximum level when an On command is received by an On/Off cluster on the same endpoint.

§ ATTRID_LEVEL_REMAINING_TIME

#define ATTRID_LEVEL_REMAINING_TIME   0x0001

The RemainingTime attribute represents the time remaining until the current command is complete - it is specified in 1/10ths of a second.

§ ATTRID_LEVEL_START_UP_CURRENT_LEVEL

#define ATTRID_LEVEL_START_UP_CURRENT_LEVEL   0x4000

The StartUpCurrentLevel attribute SHALL define the desired startup level vfor a device when it is supplied with power and this level SHALL be reflected in the CurrentLevel attribute.

§ COMMAND_LEVEL_MOVE

#define COMMAND_LEVEL_MOVE   0x01

On receipt of this command, a device SHALL move from its current level in an up or down direction in a continuous fashion.

§ COMMAND_LEVEL_MOVE_TO_CLOSEST_FREQUENCY

#define COMMAND_LEVEL_MOVE_TO_CLOSEST_FREQUENCY   0x08

Upon receipt of this command, the device shall change its current frequency to the requested frequency, or to the 4785 closest frequency that it can generate.

§ COMMAND_LEVEL_MOVE_TO_LEVEL

#define COMMAND_LEVEL_MOVE_TO_LEVEL   0x00

On receipt of this command, a device SHALL move from its current level to the value given in the Level field.

§ COMMAND_LEVEL_MOVE_TO_LEVEL_WITH_ON_OFF

#define COMMAND_LEVEL_MOVE_TO_LEVEL_WITH_ON_OFF   0x04

The Move to Level (with On/Off), command have identical payload to the Move to Level command. Before commencing any command that has the effect of setting the CurrentLevel above the minimum level allowed by the device, the OnOff attribute of the On/Off cluster on the same endpoint, if implemented, SHALL be set to On. If any command that has the effect of setting the CurrentLevel to the minimum level allowed by the device, the OnOff attribute of the On/Off cluster on the same endpoint, if implemented, SHALL be set to Off.

§ COMMAND_LEVEL_MOVE_WITH_ON_OFF

§ COMMAND_LEVEL_STEP

#define COMMAND_LEVEL_STEP   0x02

On receipt of this command, a device SHALL move from its current level in an up or down direction.

§ COMMAND_LEVEL_STEP_WITH_ON_OFF

§ COMMAND_LEVEL_STOP

#define COMMAND_LEVEL_STOP   0x03

Upon receipt of this command, any Move to Level, Move or Step command (and their 'with On/Off' variants) currently in process SHALL be terminated.
